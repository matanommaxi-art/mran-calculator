How the Calculator Works
1
Convert Weight to Kilograms
Actual weight must be in kilograms. If your weight is in pounds, divide by 2.20462 to convert.
kg = lbs ÷ 2.20462
2
Calculate Volumetric Weight
Volumetric weight accounts for the size of your package. Enter dimensions in inches.
Volumetric Weight (kg) = (L × W × H in inches) ÷ 366
3
Chargeable Weight
The chargeable weight is the higher of the actual weight or volumetric weight. Airlines always bill on the greater of the two.
Chargeable Weight = MAX(actual weight, volumetric weight)
4
Air Cost Calculation
Our air freight to Kenya rate is $15/KG based on the actual or volumetric weight — whichever is higher. The minimum charge is $15. A flat $5 customs clearance fee is added to every shipment.
Air Cost = (Chargeable Weight × $15) + $5 customs
